Scorecard OCR

Photo to
hole data

Found a course that's not in our database? Just snap the paper scorecard. Gemini AI reads every column and loads all 18 holes — par, yardage, stroke index, and tee ratings — in seconds.

  • 📷
    One photo, full scorecard Captures all tee boxes, course rating, and slope rating automatically.
  • ✏️
    Editable review table Tap any cell to correct a digit before saving — you're always in control.
  • ☁️
    Saved for everyone Once uploaded, the course is available to all TeeStats users instantly.
  • 🔁
    Auto-detected 9-hole courses If front and back 9 are identical, TeeStats automatically treats it as a 9-hole layout.
